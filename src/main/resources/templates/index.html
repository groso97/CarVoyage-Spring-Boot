A deductible is the maximum amount of participation in damage. The amount of the deductible depends on the vehicle category.
CDW stands for Collision Damage Waiver - a risk fee with a deductible for damage, varying by vehicle groups.
TP stands for Theft Protection - a risk fee for the theft of the vehicle with a maximum theft deductible, varying by vehicle groups.
CDW and TP are always included in the rental price.
CDR stands for Collision Damage Reduction – Participation in damage (deductible) can be partially waived by paying an additional fee for partial deductible waiver according to the current price list, in which case the user pays a reduced deductible amount, varying by vehicle groups.
